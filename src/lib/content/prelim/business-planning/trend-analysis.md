!!Trend analysis!! is a process of **investigating changes over time** and looking for a **pattern** in order to **predict the future**. It will provide more **reliable information** on which an SME owner can base **decisions**, while helping make **forecasting more accurate**.

When trend analysis is used properly, it will assist SME owners to achieve business **success** by helping **forecast** such factors as:

- potential sales
- total revenue
- total operating costs
- gross and net profits
- availability of labour
