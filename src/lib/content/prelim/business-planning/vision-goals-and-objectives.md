## Vision

!!Vision statement:!! a broad statement about what the business **aspires to become in the future**.

The main purpose of a vision statement is to **guide and direct** the business owners, managers and employees. It creates the **culture** within the business and acts as a **benchmark** against which to measure all the business's **decisions and operations**.

## Business goals

Goals are **motivating forces** behind the business. Deciding how the goals are achieved means developing an action plan that breaks them down into objectives.

!!Objective:!! a specific statment detailing what a business **needs to achieve** in order to accomplish its vision.

!!Strategic goals:!! broad, long-term aims that apply to the business as a whole and are set by senior management.<br/>
!!Tactical objectives:!! mid-term, departmental issues.<br/>
!!Operational objectives:!! short-term issues.

Each lower goal contributes to the achieving of the **next higher level goal** (operational -> tactical -> strategic -> vision).

- A **vision** is a broad statement of overall purpose
- A **goal** is a more specific statement of what is intended to be achieved
- An **objective** is a very specific statement of how the goal should be achieve

## Long-term growth

!!Long-term growth:!! the ability of a business to **continually expand**. It is important that a business is **sustainable into the future**.
