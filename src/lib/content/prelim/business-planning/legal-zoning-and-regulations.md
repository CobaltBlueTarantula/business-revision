## Business name

Business owners need to register a business name to prohibit anyone else from trading with the same name.

## Zoning

The **local government** has the authority to restrict where certain businesses can locate. Each council has a **local planning scheme** and land is **zoned for particular uses**, such as residential, commercial, industrial, agricultural or recreational.

Businesses must inquire with the **local council** to determine which **zoning regulations** will affect the business now and in the future.

## Health regulations

Each local government provides businesses with the **requirements and standards** to meet in order to receive a **licence to operate** (primarily those dealing with food).

## Competition and consumer act

Designed to **protect consumers**. Enforced by the ACCC (see [Institutional influence](../nature-of-business/external-influences)).
