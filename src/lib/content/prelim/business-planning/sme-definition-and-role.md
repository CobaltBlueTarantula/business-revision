An SME is a business with **fewer than 200 employees**. Reasons for wanting to stay small include avoiding pressures and difficulties of larger businesses, being content with the current size, or being able to offer personalised service.

<img src="/images/business-size-classification.jpg" alt="Business size classifications according to the ABS" width="500" />

Australian SMEs:

- Employ over half of all people working in the private sector
- Produce over half of all products each year
- Account for almost half of all money spent on **Research and Development**
- Earn more profits and pay more taxes than larger businesses
