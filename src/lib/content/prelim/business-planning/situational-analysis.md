## SWOT Analysis

### Internal Assessment

Given that a business has control over its internal environment, an internal analysis will provide information that can help the business improve its financial position and set new goals.

**Strengths:** customer loayalty, product popularity, skilled and motivated workforce, efficient operations, financial position, new technology and equipment.<br/>
**Weaknesses:** staff and management competency, obsolete technology, past failures, facility upgrading.

### External Assessment

An external analysis is particularly important to a business plan when identifying unsatisfied demand that the business can satisfy. The business owner should also attempt to convert threats into opportunities using an external analysis.

**Opportunities:** new technology, national economy strength, low interest rates, possible new markets, potential acquisitions.<br/>
**Threats:** evident trends in markets, new laws regulating operations, new competitiors, loss of market share to competition.
