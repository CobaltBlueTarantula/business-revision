## Success of SMEs

5 Keys to SME success:

1. !!Entrepreneural abilities:!! some owners will have drive and enthusiasm, set targets or take big risks, as well as **enjoying the process of solving a problem**.
2. !!Access to information:!! accurate and up-to-date information will allow the SME owner to make **better decisions**. Therefore, SME owners should have information regarding **profiatability**, **quality** of products and services, and the **performance of employees**.
3. !!Flexibility:!! the small size of the business allowes the owner to **adapt quickly to changes in the external environment**. Decisions can be made quickly due to **fewer levels of management**. Additionally, close owner-customer relationships can help the business respond quickly to **changing tastes and preferences**.
4. !!Focus on market niche:!! small businesses can attempt to fulfil the needs of a **small market of consumers**, providing the SME with **strong relationships** and **specialisation**.
5. !!Reputation:!! servicing a **narrow market niche** allows the development of of a **reputation for quality and service**.

## Failure of SMEs

Top reasons for SME failure:

1. Insufficient leadership and management
2. Insufficient planning and execution
3. Inadequate market research, marketing, sales
4. Poor financial management
5. Underestimating the impact of externalities
