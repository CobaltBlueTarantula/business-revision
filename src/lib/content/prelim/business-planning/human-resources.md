## Skills

Skilled employees are **highly productive** and result in creating **wealth for the business**.

## On-costs

!!On-costs:!! payments for non-wage benefits.

The main on-costs include:

- work health and safety requirements
- long service leave
- sick leave
- superannuation
- holiday pay
- study leave
- maternity and paternity leave
- workers' compensation
- leave loading

!!Superannuation:!! requires all employers to make a financial contribution to a fund that employees can access when they leave or retire from a job.
