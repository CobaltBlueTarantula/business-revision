## Management as planning, organising and controlling

!!Planning:!! deciding on the **direction the business will take** in the future, then **setting goals and objectives**.

!!Organising:!! creating a framework for the **implmentation** of the business plan, determining **what** is to be done, **who** should do it and **how** it will be done.

!!Controlling:!! comparing the results achieved with what was planned.

## Hierarchical organisational structure

- Division of labour, specialisation, increased efficiency
- Long chain of command, narrow span of control

## Autocratic leadership style

- Decisions passed down the chain, information passed up
- Managers made all decisions without employee involvement
- Allows quick implementation of decisions and changes
