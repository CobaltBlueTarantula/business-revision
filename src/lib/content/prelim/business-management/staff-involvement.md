Participative styles of management involve staff in decision-making and give them a feeling of **inclusiveness and value to the business**. Managers can use interpersonal and communication skills to increasingly involve staff in the business operations. This involvement is often a very important **source of motivation**.

Often staff members are a **source of innovations** that can solve a difficult problem or give the business a **competitive advantage**.

!!Mentoring:!! a method of **informal training** whereby a less knowledgeable and **less experienced employee** is guided by a more **senior member of staff**. Mentoring provides a sense of **value, empowerment and confidence** to the employee so they can help achieve the goals of the business. This might also **intrinsically motivate the mentor**.
