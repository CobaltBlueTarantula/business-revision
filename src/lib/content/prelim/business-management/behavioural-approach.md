## Management as leading, motivating and communicating

!!Leading:!! encourages workers so that the business **achieves its goals and objectives**.

!!Motivating:!! **monetary and non-monetary rewards** that influence individuals to achieve goals.

!!Communicating:!! the exchange of information between people, supporting thw **two-way flow of information** through feedback and **shared decision-making**.

## Teams organisational structure

- The traditional hierarchical structure has flattened out
- Fewer levels in the chain of command and improved communication
- Increased flexibility and responsiveness to change

## Democratic leadership style

- Employees are encouraged to share opinions and suggestions
- Managers discuss the situation with people involved
- Input from employees is considered by managers
- Collaborative nature that supports leadership
