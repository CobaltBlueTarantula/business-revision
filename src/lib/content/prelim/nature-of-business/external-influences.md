## Competitive situation

Competitive situation is influenced by the number of competitors and the ease with which a business can enter a particular market.

### Ease of entry

Ease of entry is based on:

- Cost of establishing the business (financial resources)
- Access tomaterials (availability of inputs)

### Market situations

**Monopoly:** only one firm in the marketplace.<br/>
**Oligopoly:** a limited number of suppliers to the market, meaning there is less pressure on businesses to offer low prices and innovate.

**Perfect competition:** many small firms sell identical products to many buyers, and no single entity can influence prices, which are determined by supply and demand.<br/>
**Monopolistic competition:** many firms sell products that are similar but differentiated, allowing them some control over pricing. Combines elements of both monopoly and perfect competition.
